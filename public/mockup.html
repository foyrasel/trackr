✅ Redesign Decisions

  • One hero number: net monthly position
  • Pill nav — 5 items max, centered, always visible
  • FAB (+) always bottom-right — one clear action
  • Branches & leaves for history (months = branches)
  • Budget = branch capacity bars with leaf-tip indicator
  • Insight card with money-tree anatomy SVG
  • Telegram card in Settings — clear setup path
  • PWA banner: top, dismissible, one-time
